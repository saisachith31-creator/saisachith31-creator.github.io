Hysent International (China) Limited, headquartered in Hong Kong, is dedicated to promoting premium quality equipment and products in marine & offshore segments, having offices located in China’s Shanghai, Dalian and Japan’s Osaka.

Professional technical supports and prompt services are our promises we made to customers. After years of rapid development, we have already forged solid cooperation with the majority of shipyards in China and ship-owners all over the world.

Integrity, Quality, and Best Service are our philosophy. We always believe that integrity is the fundamental for long-term cooperation, quality reliable is the key to customers’ trust, and professional & prompt services is the clients’ satisfaction.

Representing the integrity of business ethics, the first-class quality, and the best service, providing the strongest support for all the parties, fulfills the win-win and common development with our customers, rewarding our employees and society at most.
