Marine Upholstery

Product Overview

Marine Upholstery solutions are specialized interior furnishing systems designed to provide comfort, durability, and aesthetic enhancement in marine and offshore environments. These include seating, wall panels, cushions, mattresses, and other soft furnishing components tailored for use on vessels and offshore installations.

Engineered to withstand harsh marine conditions, marine upholstery materials are resistant to moisture, salt exposure, UV radiation, and wear. In addition to comfort and visual appeal, these solutions are developed with safety in mind, incorporating fire-retardant materials that comply with marine safety regulations.

Marine upholstery plays a key role in enhancing onboard living and working environments, ensuring both functionality and long-term performance under demanding conditions.

Advantages

Marine Upholstery enhances onboard comfort while maintaining durability in challenging marine conditions. Its moisture-resistant and UV-stable properties ensure long-lasting performance with minimal degradation over time.

The use of fire-retardant and safety-compliant materials ensures adherence to marine regulations, making it suitable for both commercial and offshore applications. With customizable designs, it also improves the aesthetic appeal of interiors while maintaining practicality and ease of maintenance.

Certifications & Standards

  • IMO (International Maritime Organization) fire safety compliance
  • ISO-certified manufacturing processes
  • Marine-grade material standards
  • Fire-retardant and low flame-spread certification
  • Compliance with offshore and marine interior safety regulations

Maintenance & Service

Marine Upholstery requires routine cleaning and inspection to maintain hygiene, appearance, and durability. Periodic checks for wear, tear, and fastening integrity are recommended, especially in high-usage areas. Proper maintenance ensures extended lifespan and continued compliance with safety standards.
