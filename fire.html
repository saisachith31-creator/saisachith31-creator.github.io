Fire & Blast Doors

Product Overview

Fire & Blast Doors are high-performance protective doors designed to withstand extreme fire exposure, blast pressure, and hazardous environmental conditions. These doors are engineered to protect personnel, critical equipment, and structural integrity by preventing the spread of fire, smoke, and explosive forces between compartments.

They are commonly installed in marine vessels, offshore platforms, petrochemical plants, industrial facilities, and high-risk infrastructure where both fire safety and blast resistance are required. These doors act as a vital barrier in emergency situations, ensuring compartmentalization and safe evacuation routes.

Constructed using heavy-duty fire-resistant and reinforced materials, fire & blast doors are designed to maintain structural stability under intense thermal and pressure loads while complying with strict international safety standards.
