Fire & Blast Doors

Product Overview

Fire & Blast Doors are high-performance protective doors designed to withstand extreme fire exposure, blast pressure, and hazardous environmental conditions. These doors are engineered to protect personnel, critical equipment, and structural integrity by preventing the spread of fire, smoke, and explosive forces between compartments.

They are commonly installed in marine vessels, offshore platforms, petrochemical plants, industrial facilities, and high-risk infrastructure where both fire safety and blast resistance are required. These doors act as a vital barrier in emergency situations, ensuring compartmentalization and safe evacuation routes.

Constructed using heavy-duty fire-resistant and reinforced materials, fire & blast doors are designed to maintain structural stability under intense thermal and pressure loads while complying with strict international safety standards.

Advantages

Fire & Blast Doors provide dual protection against fire hazards and explosive pressure, making them essential in high-risk environments. Their robust construction ensures containment of fire and blast effects, significantly reducing damage spread and improving survival safety.

These doors are engineered for reliability under extreme conditions, ensuring they remain functional during emergencies. Their corrosion-resistant and high-strength design makes them suitable for long-term use in harsh marine and industrial environments.

Certifications & Standards

  • Complies with international fire resistance standards

  • ISO-certified manufacturing processes
  • Marine and offshore safety compliance standards
  • Tested for structural integrity under extreme conditions

Maintenance & Service

Fire & Blast Doors require regular inspection to ensure sealing integrity, hinge performance, and locking mechanisms remain fully functional. Periodic testing of fire and blast resistance systems is recommended to maintain compliance with safety regulations and ensure emergency readiness.
