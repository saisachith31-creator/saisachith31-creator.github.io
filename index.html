Our Operations

HYI China

Hysent International (China) Ltd manages core manufacturing, engineering, and project execution. With offices in Dalian and Shanghai, the China team supports large-scale industrial and marine projects across global markets. Additionally, the China division is responsible for product development, quality assurance, and technical support, ensuring all solutions meet international standards and project requirements.

HYI Singapore

Hysent International Pte Ltd focuses on sales, customer support, and technical coordination in Singapore. It serves as a key hub for Southeast Asia operations, providing localized service and faster response to clients. The Singapore office also works closely with the China team to coordinate project execution, strengthen client relationships, and support regional business development.

HYI Solution

Hysent provides a wide range of ventilation and engineering solutions including axial fans, centrifugal fans, tunnel fans, smoke extraction systems, and customized industrial applications. These solutions are designed to deliver high performance, energy efficiency, and reliability across various industries such as marine, infrastructure, and industrial facilities.
