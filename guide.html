When to Use Waterless Car Care

Waterless car care products are best used when your vehicle has light to moderate dirt, dust, or fingerprints. They are ideal for quick clean-ups, apartment living, or areas where traditional washing is not convenient.

Best Surfaces

SwiftShine works best on painted surfaces, clear coat finishes, glass, and smooth exterior panels.

When Not to Use

Avoid using waterless products on heavily muddy vehicles or surfaces with thick sand or grit, as this may cause scratches.

Clean one panel at a time to maintain control and ensure even results across the vehicle.
