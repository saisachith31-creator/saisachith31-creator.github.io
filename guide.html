Product Usage Guide

This guide provides practical advice, best practices, and safety tips to help you get the most out of your SwiftShine products.

When to Use SwiftShine

SwiftShine is designed for light to moderate surface dirt and dust. It is best used for maintenance cleaning rather than heavy washing.

  • Daily dust and fingerprints
  • Light road grime
  • Quick clean-ups between washes
  • Indoor or apartment parking areas

SwiftShine products are suitable for a wide range of exterior surfaces.

Microfiber Towel Tips

Towels play a major role in achieving good results.

  • Use clean, high-quality microfiber towels
  • Fold towel into sections and rotate frequently
  • Never reuse a towel that has fallen on the ground

Frequently Asked Questions

Will this scratch my car?

When used correctly on lightly dirty surfaces with clean towels, SwiftShine is safe for regular maintenance.

Can I use this in direct sunlight?

For best results, use in shaded or cooler areas to prevent product drying too quickly.

How often can I use SwiftShine?

SwiftShine can be used as frequently as needed for light cleaning and maintenance.
