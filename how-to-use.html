How to Use SwiftShine

Please take a moment to read the steps below carefully before using the product to achieve the best results.

STEP 1

Prepare the Surface

Ensure the vehicle surface is cool and free from heavy mud or debris. Use a clean microfiber cloth before starting.

STEP 2

Spray the Product

Starting from the top panels, lightly spray SwiftShine onto a small section at a time.

STEP 3

Wipe Gently

Using a microfiber cloth, wipe in one direction to lift dirt and residue. Avoid circular motions.

STEP 4

Buff to Shine

Flip to a clean side or use a second cloth to gently buff the surface to a clean shine.

STEP 5

Repeat & Finish

Repeat on all panels, bumpers, headlights, and wheels. Wash microfiber cloths after use.
